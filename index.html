Blogging

The word blog is actually a shortened form of its original name, "weblog." These weblogs allowed early internet users to "log" the details of their day in diary-style entries. Blogs often allow readers to comment, so as they became more common, communities sprung up around popular blogs. And in blogging we usually creates a blog post

Blog Post

A blog post is a form of content that is published periodically on a blog, typically with the aim of providing information, entertainment, or inspiration. In most cases, the blogger provides a short introduction to a particular topic and then launches into the main content. Blog posts are usually written in chronological order and divided into sections to make it easier for readers to follow along.
